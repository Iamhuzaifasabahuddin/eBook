Common questions

Everything you need to know

Still have questions? Our team responds to all enquiries within 4 business hours.


How long does a full publishing package take? +
Most Professional packages are completed in 3–5 weeks, depending on manuscript length and revision rounds. Starter packages typically take 7–10 business days. We also offer a 48-hour priority service for urgent projects at an additional fee.
Do I retain full copyright and royalties? +
Absolutely. You retain 100% of your copyright and all royalties. We are a services company — we never take a percentage of sales or any ongoing rights. You own everything we produce for you, including cover art and formatted files.
What file formats do you accept for the manuscript? +
We accept .docx (Microsoft Word), .pages, .rtf, .odt, and Google Docs (shared link). Most authors submit in Word format. If you have a specific format, contact us and we'll advise the best way to submit.
Can I publish exclusively on Amazon KDP? +
Yes. We support KDP Select (Kindle Unlimited exclusive) and wide distribution. We'll advise on the pros and cons of each strategy for your genre before you decide — the choice is always yours.
What if I'm not happy with the editing or design? +
All packages include at least one full revision round, and Professional includes unlimited revisions until you are satisfied. In the unlikely event you're not happy after revisions, we offer a full refund on that service — no questions asked.
Do you work with non-fiction, business books, or academic texts? +
Yes — we work across all genres and formats including business, self-help, memoir, cookbooks, children's books, academic texts, and more. Complex layouts, charts, and illustrations are handled by our specialist formatting team.
